The Firelights

The Firelights are a group of Zaunites who were dedicated to disrupting Silco's Shimmer business, and thus his control over their home. Banding together over their need to survive in the undercity without true support from Piltover, whose authorities were in Silco's pocket, the group also took in other children so as not to have them wandering around the Shimmer-infested streets.

GROUP LEADER: Ekko
